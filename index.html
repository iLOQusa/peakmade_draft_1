About the customer

PeakMade is a collection of real estate brands based in Atlanta, Georgia, that manages almost 300 properties throughout the United States. The company is committed to building communities that put people first.

Located in Boone, North Carolina, The Finmore at 241 features 175 units for approximately 575 students. The fully amenitized community includes a resort-style hot tub, a variety of fitness rooms and a coffee bar.

About the project

With frequent resident turnover, property managers had struggled to maintain clear visibility into who had access to specific units and at what times. Constantly purchasing and replacing lock batteries added unnecessary cost and effort, and residents often faced access issues whenever the Internet went down.

The solution

Following the initial successful retrofit of the units with iLOQ’s 5 Series smart access solution, the property decided to go one step further and upgrade the solution to the iLOQ 5 Series+ platform. The new iLOQ App enhanced the user experience for the residents, while the iLOQ Manager+ cloud-based programming solution and browser-based iLOQ Web improved administration for the property managers.

How everyday life became smarter and more efficient for The Finmore at 241

01 Fast retrofit

iLOQ’s cylinders are quick and easy to retrofit. There is no need for special tools or drilling and no need to change the doors, locks or existing infrastructure.

02 Easy administration

As new residents join or leave, their access credentials can be quickly and easily added or cancelled in the iLOQ Manager+ cloud-based administration software. For daily access management tasks, the browser-based iLOQ Web offers a convenient and effective solution alongside iLOQ Manager+.

03 Enhanced security

Property Managers have visibility and control at all times over who has access to what units and when.

04 Best-in-class support

iLOQ has acted as a true partner to The Finmore at 241, helping the property team solve access challenges and unlock greater efficiency in daily operations. With reliable support and practical guidance, iLOQ has helped make access management simpler, faster and more convenient for the team.
